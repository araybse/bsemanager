BSE Manager Launch Narrative: Team Announcement Script
======================================================

Use this as source text for NotebookLM video generation.
Tone target: high-energy, confident, practical, forward-looking.

Opening hook
------------
"We are not just improving reports. We are building the financial operating system for BSE.
This changes how we plan, how we execute, and how we stay in control as we grow."

Narrative arc (for a 3-6 minute internal video)
------------------------------------------------
Beat 1: The old problem
- Too much critical context spread across tools, sheets, and manual reconciliation.
- Hard to move from "what happened" to "what do we do next."

Beat 2: The new model
- One connected system where accounting truth, project planning, expenses, contracts, and cash
  are visible together.
- We can now explain not just results, but drivers.

Beat 3: What people will feel immediately
- Faster answers in meetings.
- Fewer "where did this number come from?" moments.
- Better confidence in month-end and in forward plans.

Beat 4: Concrete upgrades people should know
- Cash Flow page:
  - Full matrix with drilldowns, grouped sections, and persistent forecasts.
  - Net income and bank balances are now operational planning tools.
- Contract Labor:
  - Full contract registry plus schedule and reconciliation views.
  - Project grouping and collapsible controls for cleaner review.
- Expenses page:
  - Unified list of all job-coded expenses from QBO.
  - Vendor and contract visibility.
  - Reimbursable controls, status flow, invoice linkage, paid date visibility.

Beat 5: Why leadership should care
- Better forecast quality means better decisions.
- Better linkage means cleaner billing recovery and margin protection.
- Better reconciliation means fewer surprises.

Suggested section voiceover lines
---------------------------------
On trust:
"Historical periods are grounded in source truth. Forward periods are explicit planning assumptions.
We are no longer mixing those blindly."

On contracts and expenses:
"Contract labor is no longer hidden in a generic expense bucket.
It is tracked as a managed commitment with schedule behavior and reconciliation."

On cash:
"Bank balance is no longer a disconnected number.
We can now explain month-over-month movement and isolate non-P&L effects."

On operational maturity:
"We are moving from bookkeeping visibility to management visibility."

Suggested closing
-----------------
"This is a major step toward a fully integrated operating system for BSE.
We now have stronger visibility, cleaner controls, and a better foundation for scale.
Next, we keep improving automation and forecasting precision so every decision gets better."

On-screen callouts (optional)
-----------------------------
- "Single Source + Planning Layer"
- "Contracts, Expenses, Invoices: Linked"
- "Historical Truth + Forward Control"
- "Cash Rollforward with Reconciliation"
- "Built for Scale"
