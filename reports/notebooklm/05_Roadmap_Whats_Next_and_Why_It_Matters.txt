BSE Manager Roadmap: What Is Next and Why It Matters
====================================================

Roadmap theme
-------------
We are now in the phase of compounding value:
from strong integration foundations to smarter automation and tighter planning precision.

Near-term priorities
--------------------
1) Complete invoice-expense linkage coverage
- Finish deterministic linking for remaining unlinked reimbursable rows.
- Add controlled manual-assignment workflow for edge cases.
- Maintain clear status transitions: Pending -> Invoiced -> Paid.

2) Strengthen contract-intelligence behaviors
- Improve automatic contract matching confidence with explicit rule scoring.
- Add exception reporting for unmatched or low-confidence matches.
- Surface contract consumption and remaining commitment more prominently.

3) Harden forecast governance
- Continue separating actuals from projections with clear visuals and labels.
- Add guardrails where projected totals exceed known remaining scope.
- Expand scenario support for planning volatility (conservative/base/aggressive).

4) Expand test and audit coverage
- Add integrity checks for:
  - expense-invoice link consistency
  - contract linkage drift over time
  - chart/table parity in all view modes
- Add report pack generation for monthly close and leadership review.

Medium-term opportunities
-------------------------
1) Workflow automation
- Suggested actions queue:
  - expenses needing invoice assignment
  - contracts needing review
  - forecast cells outside guardrails

2) Better operational visibility
- Alerts for exceptions and anomalies:
  - missing paid dates after status changes
  - project rows with unusual cash movement
  - non-P&L deltas exceeding threshold

3) Team-scale adoption tooling
- Role-based summary views by function:
  - leadership
  - project management
  - accounting operations

4) Planning intelligence
- Improve projection suggestions based on historical pattern + contract schedule context.
- Keep manual override as first-class control.

Success metrics to track
------------------------
- % of non-contract expenses linked to invoices
- % of contract-labor expenses linked to contracts
- Month-end reconciliation exceptions count
- Forecast variance between projected and actual outcomes
- Time to close and time to resolve exceptions

Vision checkpoint
-----------------
The long-term win is not just better reports.
It is an operating cadence where decisions are faster, assumptions are explicit, and
financial outcomes are easier to steer.

This roadmap keeps us pointed at that outcome.
