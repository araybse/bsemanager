DARK MODE SUPPORT:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Colors automatically adapt via Tailwind's dark: variants:
- Background: bg-card (light: white, dark: dark gray)
- Text: text-foreground (light: black, dark: white)
- Muted text: text-muted-foreground
- Borders: border (adapts to theme)
- Health colors remain vibrant in both modes
